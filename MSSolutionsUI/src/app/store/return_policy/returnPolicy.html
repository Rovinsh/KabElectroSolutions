Return Policy

Please review our return and exchange guidelines carefully before making a purchase.

We offer refund or exchange within the first 10 days from the date of your purchase. If 10 days have passed since your purchase, you will not be eligible for a return, exchange, or refund of any kind.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return or exchange:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as you received it.
  • The item must be returned in its original packaging.
  • Items purchased on sale or special offers may not be eligible for return or exchange.
  • Only items found defective or damaged are eligible for replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain categories of products may be exempted from returns or refunds. These categories will be clearly identified at the time of purchase.

Return / Exchange Process

For accepted return or exchange requests, once your returned item is received and inspected by us, we will notify you via email regarding the receipt of the returned product.

If the request is approved after quality inspection, your return or exchange will be processed in accordance with our policies.
