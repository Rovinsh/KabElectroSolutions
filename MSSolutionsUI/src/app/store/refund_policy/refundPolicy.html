Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines the terms for cancellations, returns, and refunds for purchases made through our platform.

This Refund and Cancellation Policy describes how you can cancel or request a refund for a product or service purchased through our Platform https://mscareprime.in.

Cancellation Policy

  1. Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 10 days of placing the order.
  2. Cancellation requests may not be entertained if the orders have already been communicated to the sellers/merchants listed on the Platform and they have initiated the shipping process or the product is out for delivery. In such cases, you may reject the product at the time of delivery.
  3. We do not accept cancellation requests for perishable items such as flowers, eatables, etc. However, refund or replacement may be provided if the user establishes that the delivered product quality is unsatisfactory.

Refund & Replacement Policy

  1. In case you receive damaged or defective items, please report the issue to our customer service team within 10 days of receiving the product. The request will be processed after verification by the seller/merchant.
  2. If the product received is not as shown on the website or does not meet your expectations, you must notify customer service within 10 days of delivery. Our team will review the complaint and take an appropriate decision.
  3. For products covered under manufacturer warranty, customers are requested to contact the manufacturer directly.

Refund Processing

In case any refund is approved by MS Care Prime, it may take up to 10 days for the refund to be processed and credited to your original payment method.
